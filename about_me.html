My Story

I’ve been fortunate to grow, learn, and thrive with the unwavering support of my parents. My journey has been anything but conventional. Born in the U.S., I spent my late childhood and early teenage years in Malaysia before returning to the U.S. in 2019—just before the pandemic—to continue my education. These multicultural experiences have shaped my adaptability, resilience, and appreciation for diverse perspectives.

Throughout my life, I’ve gravitated toward leadership and challenge because I genuinely believe in leading by example. Whether captaining my high school basketball team or spearheading a creative initiative to combat dengue mosquitoes in Malaysia, I’ve learned the importance of empathy, clear communication, and staying calm under pressure. One of my biggest motivations has always been my little brother, who dreams of becoming a professional soccer player. His ambition inspires me, and I strive every day to set a positive example for him—showing that with discipline, curiosity, and heart, anything is possible. These experiences have shaped a growth-oriented mindset and a deep sense of responsibility that I carry into every aspect of life.

Today, I’m completing my Bachelor of Science in Information Technology at the University of Kansas, and I’m actively exploring how technology can elevate every side of the sports world. I’m especially curious about the intersection of software development, data analytics, sports technology, and sports management and how digital tools can enhance athlete performance, streamline operations, improve decision-making, and create better experiences for fans, players, and organizations.

I’m still discovering where in this space I fit best, and I’m comfortable being in that exploration phase. Some areas draw me toward software engineering, others toward data analysis, athlete-development tools, or the systems that keep sports organizations running efficiently. Given the current advancements in modern day technology, I’m also fascinated by how artificial intelligence is beginning to shape the future of sports—whether through predictive analytics, advanced scouting, automated officiating support, injury-risk modeling, or personalized training insights. I want to understand not just how these tools work, but how they can be used responsibly and meaningfully.

A big part of why this journey matters to me is personal as I think a lot about athletes who are still carving out their own path like a certain someone who’s continuously chasing his dreams in soccer. Seeing his journey makes me passionate about exploring tech that helps young athletes train smarter, stay motivated, and navigate the ups and downs of growth. It reminds me that technology isn’t just about efficiency or data; it can be a powerful tool for confidence, development, and opportunity.

All of this fuels my curiosity and my willingness to stay open-minded as I continue learning. Whether I’m building software, analyzing data, experimenting with AI tools, or studying how sports organizations operate, I’m driven by the possibility of contributing to solutions that genuinely help people, athletes, coaches, and communities.

As of the moment I don’t have all the answers yet and I don’t know if that’ll ever come to be, but I’m excited about discovering where I can make the most meaningful impact in this ever evolving world of sports, technology, and innovation.

Beyond academics, I enjoy exploring new hobbies and staying active—whether I’m strategizing on the basketball court, battling it out in Uno, experimenting in the kitchen as the household sous chef, or diving into emerging tech trends. I thrive on curiosity, creativity, and meaningful conversations.

At my core, I’m a family-oriented individual driven by purpose, curiosity, and meaningful connection. I’m inspired by the people around me, especially my younger brother, and I hope to one day build or contribute to sports-tech solutions that support athletes like him as they carve their own paths. I’m excited for whatever chapter comes next in this long journey ahead and am ready to bring my energy, openness to learning, and dedication to a team that values innovation, collaboration, and long-term growth across both technology and the future of sport.
